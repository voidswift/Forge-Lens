ForgeLens v1 MUST NOT include:

- GitLab
- Bitbucket
- Mobile app
- Slack bot
- Teams bot
- TCGE
- Predictive ML
- Burnout prediction
- Marketplace
- Custom metrics
- Enterprise SSO
- Advanced RAG
- Multi-region deployment
- Kubernetes
- Microservices

These ideas are valuable.

They are intentionally postponed.

ForgeLens v1 succeeds by solving one problem exceptionally well:

Understanding the health of GitHub repositories through fast, deterministic engineering analytics.

Anything outside that mission belongs in a future milestone.
